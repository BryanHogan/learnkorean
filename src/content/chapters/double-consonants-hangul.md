## Double Consonants
The last few consonants we need to learn to know all consonants of Hangul are the double consonants.

Double Consonants:
- ㄲ
- ㄸ
- ㅃ
- ㅆ
- ㅉ

So these are all consonants in Hangul: ㄱ, ㄲ,ㄴ, ㄷ, ㄸ, ㄹ, ㅁ, ㅂ, ㅃ, ㅅ, ㅆ, ㅇ, ㅈ, ㅉ, ㅊ, ㅋ, ㅌ, ㅍ, ㅎ
